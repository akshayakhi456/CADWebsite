CADD Centre Ameerpet: Leading EduTech over 35 years

Connecting between Learning and Careers

For over three decades, CADD Centre has established itself as a trusted institution, transforming the careers of more than 2.5 million learners worldwide with its cutting-edge courses. Our recent introduction of courses in Electric Vehicle Technology and Generative AI demonstrates our commitment to equipping youth with skills for a sustainable future.

CADD Centre gained recognition for organizing Job Pakka Fair 2023, India's largest talent search event. This fair presented 15,000 job openings across various cities and industries. With 40,000 students registered and 8,000 already progressing towards their dream careers, our legacy of empowering and transforming lives continues to thrive.

Beyond the classroom, we motivate the younger generation to push boundaries and engage in sustainable development within their fields. At CADD Centre, we are dedicated to shaping the workforce of tomorrow and fostering a brighter future.
